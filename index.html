Multi-scenario financial forecasting model simulating revenue, expense, and business outcomes across base, optimistic, and pessimistic cases.

Overview

This project builds a full financial model in Python to simulate and forecast business performance across multiple scenarios. Starting from historical or assumed inputs, the model projects revenue growth, operating expenses, and net outcomes — then stress-tests those projections under different economic assumptions.

The multi-scenario approach produces a range of outcomes rather than a single forecast, giving a clearer picture of upside potential and downside risk under different business conditions.
